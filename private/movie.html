The Best Movies According to Raju

Some of my favourite movies of all-time.

Silent Voice

This anime movie is great and it roughly discusses on depression

Kahani

A thriller movie with touch of vibrant culture.The movie was shot in Kolkata.

Chicchore

A beautiful film on friendship that shows there is nothing as failure.

3 idiots

A satire to the system of Indian education. Inspired by Chetan Bhagat's Novel five point someone.

Roshomon

An interesting movie about perspective and also the so called Roshomon effect directed by the famous director Akira Kurosawa

Apu's Trilogy

It is actually a collection of three movies namely Pather Pachali,Aparajito and Apur Sansar. A must watch movie trilogy directed by Satyajit Ray.
